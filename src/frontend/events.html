National Folk Festival

November 7-9, 2025 • Downtown Jackson

The National Folk Festival is coming to Jackson, Mississippi! This iconic three-day celebration of America's living cultural traditions will transform downtown Jackson into a vibrant showcase of music, dance, crafts, and food from across the nation.

Experience authentic performances on multiple outdoor stages, explore traditional crafts in the artisan marketplace, and savor diverse cuisines from food vendors representing cultures from around the world. Best of all—admission is completely FREE!

Join us November 7-9, 2025, for an unforgettable weekend celebrating the rich tapestry of American folk traditions right here in the heart of Mississippi.
