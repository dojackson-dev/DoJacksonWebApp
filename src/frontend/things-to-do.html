Two Mississippi Museums

Mississippi History & Civil Rights

Discover Mississippi's compelling story at the Two Mississippi Museums—a breathtaking campus in downtown Jackson featuring the Museum of Mississippi History and the Mississippi Civil Rights Museum.

Walk through interactive exhibits that bring to life 15,000 years of Mississippi history, from prehistoric times to the present day. Then step into the powerful Civil Rights Museum, where eight galleries chronicle the state's pivotal role in the American civil rights movement.

These world-class museums offer an unforgettable journey through triumph and tragedy, courage and change. It's more than a museum visit—it's an experience that will stay with you long after you leave.
